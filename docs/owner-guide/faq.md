# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. How do I change the homepage hero banner?
Go to **Admin Panel** ➔ **Content** ➔ **Homepage Builder**. You can upload new editorial photography, change heading text, and update button links.

## 2. What happens when a size runs out of stock?
When stock reaches `0`, the product page automatically updates:
- The size button displays a subtle diagonal cross line.
- The item cannot be added to the cart.
- A "Notify Me When Available" button appears so customers can request alerts.

## 3. How do I publish a new Privacy Policy?
Go to **Admin Panel** ➔ **Settings** ➔ **Privacy & Cookies**. Enter a new version number (e.g. `1.1`), write a brief change summary, and click **Publish & Trigger Reconsent**. All site visitors will automatically see the consent banner again to accept updated terms.

## 4. How do I track monthly sales and top products?
Go to **Admin Panel** ➔ **Analytics** (`/admin/cdp`). You will find real-time executive charts for gross revenue, average order value, conversion funnel drop-offs, and top search queries.

## 5. Can customers shop as guest users without creating an account?
Yes! Guest users can browse, add items to their cart, and complete orders. If they create an account later, their cart and privacy choices automatically sync into their new profile.
